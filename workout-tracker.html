Social Workout Tracker

Mobile Web Application for Fitness Enthusiasts

Track. Share. Improve.

The Social Workout Tracker is a mobile web application designed to help fitness enthusiasts log their workouts, track progress, and connect with friends for motivation. Built with a focus on usability and clean design, this app makes fitness tracking social and rewarding.

Key Features:

  • Custom workout plan creation
  • Comprehensive workout logging with exercises, sets, and reps
  • Track your progress with interactive charts and analytics
  • Compare your workout plans, personal records, and stats with friends
  • Connect with friends via direct messaging
  • Mobile-optimized interface

Application Interface

Dashboard showing workout plans and progress metrics
Analytics showing progress over time with interactive graphs
Exercise logging interface with detailed tracking
Connect with friends and share workouts
View your and your friend's profiles, stats, personal records and more
Chat to your friends about you workouts
